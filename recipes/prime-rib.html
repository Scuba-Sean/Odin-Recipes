Prime Rib

This low and slow prime rib will melt in your mouth

Every holiday season, i would scramble to find the perfect prime rib recipe. Until a few years ago, i found this absolute gem. Since the first time i cooked this recipe, its become a holiday staple every year. Follow this simple recipe, and you're sure to have a hit on your hands this holiday season!

Ingredients

  • 5 pound prime rib roast
  • 2 Tbsp kosher salt
  • 2 Tbsp very soft unsalted butter
  • 2 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/8 tsp cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Place beef on a rack in a pan, and rub with salt
  2. Let beef sit in the fridge for 24 - 48 hours
  3. Remove from the fridge and let it rest for a few hours to come to room temperature
  4. Preheat the oven to 300 degrees F
  5. Keep the roast on the rack, and transfer the rack to a roasting pan
  6. Mix butter with the salt, pepper, and cayenne in a small bowl and spread onto the sides and top of the roast
  7. Add more salt to the top of the roast and pour a little water into the roasting pan
  8. Place the roast into the oven until and internal temperature of 115 to 120 degrees is reached.
  9. Remove the roast from the oven, cover with foil, and let it rest for at least 20 minutes
  10. Use some broth to deglaze the drippings from the pan, and serve alongside the roast
